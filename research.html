Discover the journey: Explore the stages of our project

SMoDS App

Our application, developed for this experiment, provides you with a detailed analysis of user's interactions through the execution of selected tasks aimed at gathering the necessary information most effectively. With an intuitive and functional interface, the app records and analyzes your interactions with the device, offering valuable insights into user's digital habits.

Furthermore, the data collected will be utilized for the training of a neural network, enhancing our understanding of user behavior in the digital realm.

Hololens 2

Our experiment leverages the immersive capabilities of the HoloLens 2 mixed reality headset to delve into the intricacies of gaze tracking. By juxtaposing the gaze tracking system integrated into the headset with the values measured by our neural network, which receives input from the front-facing camera video feed of a smartphone during usage, we aim to unravel new insights into user interaction with digital devices.
